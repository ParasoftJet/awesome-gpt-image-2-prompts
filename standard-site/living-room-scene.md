# Living Room Scene — 客厅场景图

## Scene Type
Lifestyle scene / 生活场景 — Living Room

---

## English

A photorealistic lifestyle image of a beautifully styled modern living room, with the carpet from the reference image as the centerpiece. Let the carpet's pattern, color palette, and texture inspire the entire room's aesthetic — furniture selection, wall treatment, and decorative accents should all naturally harmonize with the rug's character. The scene should feel fresh and thoughtfully designed, not a copy of the reference image. Capture the image at a natural eye-level or slight high angle, 1:1 square format, 8K resolution, soft ambient lighting, no harsh reflections, no text.

---

## 中文

一张写实风格的现代客厅生活场景图，以参考图中的地毯为画面核心。让地毯的图案、色调与材质质感引导整个房间的风格——家具、墙面处理及装饰品应与地毯自然协调。场景应感觉清新且经过精心设计，而非照搬参考图。采用自然视角或略俯的角度拍摄，1:1正方形比例，8K分辨率，柔和环境光，无强反光，无文字。
