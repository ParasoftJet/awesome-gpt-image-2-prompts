# Study Room Scene — 书房场景图

## Scene Type
Lifestyle scene / 生活场景 — Study / Home Office

---

## English

A photorealistic lifestyle image of a refined home office or study, with the carpet from the reference image as the focal point under a desk and chair. Let the rug's design and color palette inspire the room's intellectual, calm aesthetic — bookshelves, desk, lighting, and wall decor should harmonize naturally with the carpet's character. Soft layered lighting with a focused warm desk lamp and diffused ambient light. 1:1 square format, 8K resolution, clean and sophisticated, no text.

## 中文

一张写实风格的精致书房或家庭办公室生活场景图，以参考图中的地毯作为书桌椅下的视觉焦点。让地毯的设计与色调激发整体空间的知性、沉静气质——书架、书桌、灯光与墙面装饰应与地毯自然融合。柔和的层次光线，带有温暖桌灯的聚焦感与漫射环境光。1:1正方形比例，8K分辨率，干净高级，无文字。
