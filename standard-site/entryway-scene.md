## Scene Type

## English

A photorealistic lifestyle image of a welcoming home entryway, with the carpet from the reference image placed as an entrance mat near the front door. The scene should feel refined and inviting — warm-toned flooring, an open or ajar front door letting in soft natural light, with simple entryway furnishings that suit the carpet's style. Slightly elevated angle looking down toward the mat. 1:1 square format, 8K resolution, clean and elegant, no text.
