## English

A top-down overhead close-up of the carpet from the reference image, laid flat on a light-colored hardwood floor. One corner of the carpet is naturally folded back to reveal the backing material, creating a subtle contrast between the patterned pile surface and the non-slip underside. The shot highlights the carpet's texture, weave, and edge finishing. Faithfully reproduce the pattern, color, and material from the reference. Soft diffused studio light, no harsh shadows, 1:1 square format, 8K resolution, photorealistic, no text.
